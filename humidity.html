Humidity



It was unexpected that the highest concentration of locations with humidity less than 50% was clustered just north of the equator. After consulting a map, this does in fact make sense. The Sahara Desert is located in northern Africa, just north of the equator. The Arabian Peninsula also has very arid and is at a similar latitude as the Sahara Desert.
